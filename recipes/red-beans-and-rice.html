Red beans and rice is a cajun style dish full of zesty flavors, often found in Louisiana, or more specifically, New Orleans. The beautiful part of this meal is that it can be cooked low and slow in the crockpot and it requires minimal proctoring during the cooking process.

Ingredients List

  • 1lb of dry kidney beans
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 stalk of celery
  • 2-3 fresh jalapeños
  • 3-5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cajun seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1lb of conecuh sausage
  • 1 cup of white rice

Steps

  1. Dice the green pepper, celery and jalapeño and add to a bowl to the side
  2. Slice the conecuh sausage into disks roughtly 1/4" in thickness
  3. Rinse the kidney beans in a colander and add to the crockpot
  4. Next, add the diced vegetables, seasonings, and sliced sausage to the crockpot
  5. Fill the crockpot with water until the water level is about 1" above the ingredients in the pot
  6. Using a large spoon, mix all the ingredients well to ensure even distribution of seasonings and vegetables
  7. Cook on medium heat 4-6 hours or until beans are soft
  8. Stir thoroughly every hour and add water if necessary
  9. Approximately 20 minutes before the beans are done, cook the white rice using your preferred cooking method
  10. In a bowl, scoop out a serving of rice, and cover generously with the red beans and sausage from the crockpot
  11. Mix the bowl well, and enjoy
  12. Otional: for an added spicy twist, consider adding your favorite hot sauce to the final mixture
