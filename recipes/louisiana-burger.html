The Louisiana Burger is my own concoction. It's made just like a regular homemade burger, but with a Louisiana twist for added flavor and spice.

Ingredients List

  • Sesame seed bun
  • Ground Beef (90/10 blend)
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tony Chachere's seasoning salt
  • Louisiana hot sauce
  • One whole egg
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Purple Onion
  • Choice of sliced cheese

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, pepper, seasoning salt, Louisiana hot sauce, and one whole egg
  2. Mix all ingredients until evenly blended
  3. Divide the mixture up into however many burgers you want to make, and flatten the pieces into the shape of a burger patty
  4. Lightly oil a cast iron skillet and heat to a medium-high heat
  5. Add patties to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side
  6. While burgers are cooking, slice the tomato and purple onions to desired thickness
  7. For the last 30 seconds of cook time, add cheese to the patties and cover the skillet to ensure an even melting of cheese to the patties
  8. Remove the cooked patties from the skillet and set aside to rest
  9. While the patties are resting, lightly butter the top and bottom bun and place inside the skillet to toast the bun
  10. After the buns are toasted, assemble the burger
  11. Add a thin layer of mayonnaise and mustard to the top and bottom bun, spreading evenly with a butter knife
  12. Add lettuce to the bottom bun
  13. Next, add the sliced onion and pickles on top of the lettuce
  14. Place the burger patty with melted cheese on top of the onion and pickles
  15. Next, place the sliced tomato on top of the patty
  16. Finally, place the top bun on the burger
  17. Plate it up, and enjoy this delicious burn with a Louusiana twist
